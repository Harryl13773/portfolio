Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

This is the personal portfolio of Harry Lian. It collects as little data as possible. This page describes exactly what is collected and why, in plain language.

Contact form

If you use the contact form, the name, email address, and message you submit are used solely to read and respond to your inquiry. The submission is delivered to me by email via Resend (an email delivery service) and may be held temporarily on the server that powers this site. It is never sold, shared for marketing, or used for anything beyond replying to you. To have a submission deleted, email lian.000155@gmail.com.

Analytics and visit counting

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, a cookieless, privacy-first analytics tool that reports aggregate page views without tracking or identifying individual visitors. Separately, the site keeps a simple visit counter: an anonymous tally of total and per-day visits, with no personal information attached. Your IP address is used momentarily for rate limiting and standard server logs, and is not stored with any counts.

Cookies and local storage

This site sets no tracking cookies. Your browser's local storage is used for two functional things only: remembering your light/dark theme choice, and a per-session flag so a visit isn't counted twice. Both stay on your device and are never transmitted for tracking.

Infrastructure

The site is served by Cloudflare (hosting, DNS, and CDN) with an API hosted on Render and counter storage on Upstash. Like all hosting providers, these services process technical connection data (such as IP addresses) as part of operating their networks.

Changes

If the site's data practices change, this page will be updated to match.
